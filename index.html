Cane Jaggery

Cane jaggery, often referred to as "gur" in some regions, is a traditional sweetener made from concentrated sugarcane juice. It's a natural, unrefined sweetener that retains more nutrients than refined sugars

Coconut Jaggery

Coconut jaggery, known for its tropical flavor and nutritional benefits, is a delightful variation of jaggery made from coconut sap sugar. Crafted from the sap of coconut palm trees, coconut jaggery embodies the essence of the tropics

Palm Jaggery

Palm jaggery, derived from the sap of palm trees, captures the essence of nature's sweetness. Crafted through traditional methods, it preserves the rich flavor and inherent nutrients, making it a cherished natural sweetener.
